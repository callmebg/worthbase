# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct / 贡献者公约行为准则

## Our Pledge / 我们的承诺

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

我们作为成员、贡献者和领导者，承诺让社区中的每个人都能获得无骚扰的参与体验，
无论年龄、体型、可见或不可见的残障、民族、性征、性别认同与表达、经验水平、
教育程度、社会经济地位、国籍、外貌、种族、宗教信仰或性取向。

## Our Standards / 我们的标准

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language / 使用友好和包容的语言
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences / 尊重不同观点和经验
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism / 友善地接受建设性批评
- Focusing on what is best for the community / 关注社区的整体利益
- Showing empathy towards other community members / 对其他社区成员表示共情

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery / 使用性化语言或图片
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments / 恶意挑衅、侮辱或贬损性评论
- Personal or political attacks / 人身攻击或政治攻击
- Public or private harassment / 公开或私下骚扰
- Publishing others' private information without permission / 未经许可发布他人隐私信息

## Enforcement / 执行

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of
acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in
response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive,
or harmful.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the community leaders via GitHub. All complaints will be reviewed
and investigated promptly and fairly.

如有不当行为，可通过 GitHub 向社区领导者举报。所有投诉将被及时、公正地审查和处理。
